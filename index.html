Clara Fernández Labrador

I am an associate research scientist at DisneyResearch|Studios in Zürich. Previously, I pursued a joint PhD in Computer Vision at the University of Zaragoza (Spain) and the University Bourgogne (France). I also spent 7 wonderful months at CVL, ETH Zürich. Before joining Disney, I was a postdoctoral researcher at MTC, ETH Zürich.

I was featured as Woman in Computer Vision on magazine Computer Vision News. link here!
